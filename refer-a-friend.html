Terms & Conditions

In order to refer a friend you must be registered with ParcelHero.
Once any person you have referred registers with ParcelHero and sends their first shipment, we will credit your ParcelHero account with £5.
The credit cannot be withdrawn; it can only be used to purchase goods or services on ParcelHero.com.

You cannot make multiple referrals from the same household.
The £50 bonus will be paid to your PayPal account or direct into your bank account when your tenth referral (and every ten after that) make their first delivery.
